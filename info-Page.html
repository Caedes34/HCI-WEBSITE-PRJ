PLANK

The plank is a core-strengthening exercise that targets the abdominal muscles, lower back, and shoulders. It helps improve posture, stability, and endurance.

⏳ 45 mins | Core | Shoulders, glutes, legs

Instructions

  1. Start in a push-up position with your elbows bent at 90 degrees, resting your weight on your forearms.
  2. Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  3. Engage your core, squeeze your glutes, and keep your neck neutral.
  4. Hold the position for as long as possible while maintaining proper form.
  5. Cover and let simmer for 30-35 minutes until chicken is tender and fully cooked.
  6. Slowly lower yourself to rest and repeat as needed.
